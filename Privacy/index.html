MangaDen Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Introduction

MangaDen ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our manga and comic reader application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

MangaDen does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not require account creation or personal details to use the app.

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your manga and comic library information
  • Downloaded chapters and reading progress
  • App preferences and settings
  • Reading history and bookmarks

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Web Browsing

When using the built-in browser to discover new content:

  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not collect URLs you visit
  • Browser sessions are temporary and not stored
  • We only process page content locally to extract manga/comic information when you choose to add titles to your library

Downloads

When you download chapters for offline reading:

  • Downloaded content is stored locally on your device
  • We do not track what content you download
  • Downloaded files are accessible only within the app
  • You can delete downloaded content at any time through the app

Third-Party Services

MangaDen may access third-party websites through the built-in browser to help you discover content. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device updated and using appropriate security features.

Children's Privacy

MangaDen does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app is designed to operate without collecting user data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, you have full control over:

  • All content stored in the app
  • Your reading preferences and history
  • Downloaded chapters
  • App settings and configurations

You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app from your device.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please don't hesitate to contact our support team directly.
