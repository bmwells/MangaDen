Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download manga for offline reading?

Tap the download icon next to any chapter. Downloaded content can be accessed from your library under "Downloads".

Is MangaDen free to use?

Yes! MangaDen offers free access to our entire library with optional premium features for enhanced experience.

How often is new content added?

We update our library daily with new chapters and series as they're released in Japan.

Can I sync my progress across devices?

Yes! Create an account and your reading progress, bookmarks, and library will sync automatically.
